Safety Information

At Buggy Ride DXB, your safety is our highest priority. Every desert adventure is carefully supervised by our professional guides to ensure you enjoy a thrilling yet completely safe dune buggy experience in Dubai. Before you take the wheel, please review the key safety rules and procedures below:

Pre-Ride Preparation

  • Every tour begins with a comprehensive safety briefing and driving orientation.
  • First-time riders are welcome — our experts provide full guidance before the ride.
  • All participants must sign a safety waiver form prior to departure.
  • Carry a valid ID or passport copy for verification and insurance purposes.

Driving Conduct in the Desert

  • Follow your lead guide at all times never overtake or leave the designated path.
  • Maintain a safe distance between buggies to avoid collisions.
  • Drive at a controlled speed, especially when climbing or descending dunes.
  • Be mindful of the terrain; sudden turns on steep dunes can cause rollovers.
  • No reckless driving or drifting outside the supervised area.

Safety Restrictions

  • Alcohol or drug use before or during the ride is strictly prohibited.
  • Pregnant women and guests with heart, back, or neck are advised not to participate.
  • Children under 10 years oldcannot join as passengers for safety reasons.
  • The maximum weight limit per seat should not exceed the manufacturer’s recommendation (usually around 120 kg).

During the Ride

  • Keep both hands on the steering wheel and both feet inside the vehicle at all times.
  • In case of any issue, stop and signal your guide immediately; never try to fix the buggy yourself.
  • Avoid sudden braking or turning on soft sand; it may cause the buggy to skid or flip.
  • Stay hydrated and take breaks as advised by your guide.
