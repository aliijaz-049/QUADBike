Planning a thrilling desert safari dubai adventure ride through the desert on a quad bike? Here’s some important information to ensure you have a safe, fun, and unforgettable experience on your Quad bike rental tours in Dubai.

Safety Guidelines for Quad Biking:

  • Always wear a helmet and follow the instructions given by your guide.
  • Keep a safe distance from other riders.
  • Avoid sharp turns at high speeds to prevent accidents.

What to Bring:

  • Water bottle, sunscreen, and your camera or smartphone to capture the stunning desert views.
  • Don't forget to carry some cash or a credit card if you plan to buy any souvenirs or snacks.

Know about Our Essential Gear for Safety:

We provide you with all the necessary gear for your safety during your quad biking adventure in the Dubai desert. We'll give you a helmet for head protection, goggles to shield your eyes from the sand, gloves for a secure grip, and sturdy shoes to keep your feet protected.

What to Wear for Quad Biking in Dubai?

We recommend/advise wearing light, breathable clothing that covers your arms and legs to protect you from the sun and any potential scrapes. Avoid loose clothing that could get caught in the bike. Comfort and safety are our top priorities for your desert adventure.

Weather Considerations:

If the weather isn't suitable on the day of your booking, don't worry—we've got you covered. We'll promptly inform you and work with you to reschedule your ride to the next available day when conditions are ideal, so you have the best experience possible without any hassle.
