We are proud to share our story with you. Founded by ESA Tours in 2023, our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to spread our love for the stunning deserts of the UAE and share that joy with adventure seekers from all over. As passionate explorers of the Dubai desert, we recognized the need for a fun and safe environment where people could experience the thrill of quad biking while appreciating the breathtaking beauty of the vast sands. With that in mind, Quad Bike Dubai was born!
